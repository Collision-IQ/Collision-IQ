**Before:**

**After 90 days on Collision IQ:**

📉 **Cycle time: 12.4 → 10.6 days (−1.8 days)**

📈 **Estimates per writer per day: +22%**

🎯 **Fewer missed operations at first write**
Included ops, refinish time, one-time-use parts — caught at photo intake, not at supplement #3.
