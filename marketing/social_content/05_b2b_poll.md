## Hook (first line, under 125 chars)

> Estimators: which carrier rule costs you the most time per file? Be honest.

You write what the repair needs. The rule-set says otherwise. Now you're spending 40 minutes documenting something you could see in 4 seconds.

We're building tooling around exactly this friction, so I want real answers, not polite ones.

**Which one burns the most hours in your shop?**
